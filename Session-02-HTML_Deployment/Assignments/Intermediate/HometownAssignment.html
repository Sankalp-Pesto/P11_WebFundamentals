RobinsVille, USA

Location

Robbinsville Township is a township in Mercer County, in the U.S. state of New Jersey.

About

Robbinsville (then known as Washington Township) was formed by an act of the New Jersey Legislature in March, 1859 after a group of independent-minded citizens petitioned to separate from East Windsor Township.

Weather

According to the Köppen climate classification system, Robbinsville Township, New Jersey has a humid subtropical climate (Cfa). Cfa climates are characterized by all month having an average mean temperature > 32.0 °F (≤ 0.0 °C), at least four months with an average mean temperature ≥ 50.0 °F (≥ 10.0 °C), at least one month with an average mean temperature ≥ 71.6 °F (≥ 22.0 °C), and no significant precipitation difference between seasons. The township lies in the coldest end of this climate zone.

Population

Robbinsville Township is 20.491 square miles and includes the Village of Windsor in Mercer County, New Jersey. Its current population according to the 2020 US Census is 15,476 with a median age of 39.1 years.
