Linkin Park Fan Webpage

Linkin Park is an American rock band formed in 1996 in Agoura Hills, California. The band rose to international fame with their debut album "Hybrid Theory," which was released in 2000. The album featured a fusion of alternative rock, nu-metal, and rap rock, and it sold over 10 million copies in the United States alone.

The band's members included vocalist Chester Bennington, guitarists Brad Delson and Mike Shinoda, bassist Dave "Phoenix" Farrell, DJ and programmer Joseph Hahn, and drummer Rob Bourdon. The band continued to release successful albums throughout the 2000s and 2010s, with a total of seven studio albums and numerous awards and nominations.

Tragically, Chester Bennington passed away in 2017, leading to the end of the band. However, their music continues to be beloved by fans around the world and their legacy as one of the most successful and influential rock bands of the 21st century remains strong. Some of their most well-known songs include "Numb," "In the End," "Crawling," "What I've Done," and "Breaking the Habit".
